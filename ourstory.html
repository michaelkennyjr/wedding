Our Story

We had our first date on February 15, 2014 after the algorithms of the internet brought us together. Since then, we've fallen in love, traveled to 17 states and two countries, eaten countless pizzas, and run 26.2 miles together. On February 11, 2018, Michael popped the question with a 10-minute serenade of love songs and scrapbook pages filled with four years of memories hung from the ceiling.

We are best friends, loving companions, and partners for life. We can't wait to get married and celebrate the start of this next chapter with our family and friends!
